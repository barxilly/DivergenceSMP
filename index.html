Our Story

A Bold Pivot Into Dairy Excellence

For several years, Divergence was a bog-standard Minecraft SMP. Our players built structures, formed lasting friendships, and definitely never griefed each other.

Then, in early 2026, our founder had an epiphany while crafting her daily milk bucket: "What if we made this real?"

Today, our state-of-the-art cheese caves (formerly Kat's house) produce over 2,000 wheels of artisan cheese annually. Each variety is named after the Minecraft memories we hold dear, and aged using proprietary techniques developed by our former redstone engineers.

We may have left the game, but we brought the craft with us.
