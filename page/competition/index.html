Divergence SMP Fan Competition

To commemorate the end of Season One of Divergence SMP, we are hosting a fan competition!

This competition comes in three parts: Fan Art Competition, Best Moments Competition, and Fan Build Competition.

The winners of each competition will receive a special role in the Discord server, as well as another special prize!

Fan Art Competition

The Fan Art Competition is a competition where you can submit your best fan art of the Divergence SMP!

The fan art can be anything, from a sculpture to a song! Or something like this amazing piece of art by Koffi:

Best Moments Competition

The Best Moments Competition is a competition where you can submit the funniest clips / screenshots from the Divergence SMP!

You might have to do some digging to find the best moments, but it will be worth it!

Fan Build Competition

The Fan Build Competition is a competition where you can submit your best builds of the Divergence SMP!

The builds can be anything, from a recreation of the spawn to a mini recreation of the entire world!

The builds will be judged on creativity, detail, and overall quality.

How to Submit

To submit your entry, you must join the Divergence SMP Discord server and submit your entry in the specified #fan-competition channel.

For the Fan Art Competition, you must submit your entry as an attachment (png, jpg, gif, etc.).

For the Best Moments Competition, you can submit your entry as an attachment (png, jpg, gif, etc.) or as a link to a clip/video.

For the Fan Build Competition, you can preferably submit your entry as a .mcworld file / link to a realm, but you can also submit your entry as an image or video.

You can submit as many entries as you want, but you can only win one competition, and more than one entry will not increase your chances of winning. Remember, quality over quantity!

Rules

  • You must be a member of the Divergence SMP Discord server to enter.
  • You can only win one competition.
  • You must submit your entry before Season Two starts on the 1st of March 2024.
  • You can submit as many entries as you want, but more than one entry will not increase your chances of winning.

By entering the Divergence SMP Fan Competition, you agree to the Terms and Conditions.

Judging

The entries will be judged mainly by me (Silly), but I will also get some help from the other members of the Divergence SMP.

The entries will be judged on creativity, detail, and overall quality.

The winners will be announced on the Divergence SMP Discord server on the 15th of March 2024. The winners will also be contacted directly.

Prizes

The winners of each competition will receive a special (Yet to be decided) role in the Divergence SMP Discord server.

The winners of each competition will also receive a chosen prize from the following list:

  • A gift card for a free copy of Minecraft Java and Minecraft Bedrock.
  • A gift card for 1720 Minecoins.
  • A gift card for 3 months of Minecraft Realms Plus (Redeemable on Minecraft Bedrock).

The winners will be able to choose one of the prizes from the list above.

There will also be a special (secret) prize for whichever entry we think is the best overall!
